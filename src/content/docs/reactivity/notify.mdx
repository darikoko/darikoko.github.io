The reactivity in prune is super simple.

If you want to update the DOM after the execution of a method, just decorate it with `@notify` and the DOM will be updated <u>only once at the end of the method</u>.

<Aside type="note">
Here the increment method will update the DOM but not the decrement method (because @notify is missing on decrement).

Even if the DOM is not updated with decrement, the store is still updated.
</Aside>
